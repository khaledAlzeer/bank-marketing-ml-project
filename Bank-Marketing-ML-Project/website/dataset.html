📋 Dataset Overview

Where the data comes from and what it represents

The dataset is sourced from the UCI Machine Learning Repository and relates to direct marketing campaigns (phone calls) of a Portuguese banking institution.

The goal is to predict whether a client will subscribe to a term deposit (binary target: yes / no). The dataset reflects a real business challenge — a heavily imbalanced target where most customers say NO.

This dataset is widely used in ML research and competitions. It was collected between May 2008 and November 2010.

Original Dataset

SMOTE Applied

✅ After SMOTE

Training Dataset (Balanced)

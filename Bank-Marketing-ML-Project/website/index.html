🎓 Machine Learning Project — ZUJ

Will the Customer Say Yes?

A real-world machine learning study on a Portuguese bank's telemarketing campaign — predicting term deposit subscriptions to save time, money, and effort.

View Roadmap See Results

📖 The Story

Where it all started…

💸

The Problem

A bank in Portugal was running phone-based marketing campaigns to get customers to subscribe to a term deposit. Agents called thousands of people every day — most of them said NO.

Every unnecessary call wasted time, money, and agent energy. The bank was spending heavily on a campaign with no real targeting strategy — just calling everyone and hoping for the best.

❌ ~88% of calls ended with no subscription
🤔

The Question

What if the bank could predict — before making the call — whether a customer is likely to say YES?

Instead of calling 10,000 people randomly, they could focus only on the right customers at the right time — saving a massive amount of resources.

💡 Smarter targeting = less cost, more success
🎯

The Goal

We built a Machine Learning system that analyzes customer data — age, job, balance, previous interactions — and predicts the likelihood of subscribing to a term deposit.

This means the bank can now prioritize calls, reduce wasted effort, and ultimately save money while increasing revenue.

🏆 Up to 88.2% prediction accuracy achieved

💰 Why Does This Matter?

Real impact. Real savings.

📞

Fewer Wasted Calls

Instead of cold-calling thousands of random customers, the bank targets only those predicted to say YES — dramatically reducing call volume.

💵

Lower Campaign Costs

Every skipped unnecessary call saves agent time and operational costs. Smarter campaigns mean a higher ROI with less spending.

📈

Higher Success Rate

By focusing on likely subscribers, the bank increases its conversion rate — more deposits, more revenue, happier customers.
